# Foozi Content Strategy

This document defines the content generation strategy for the Foozi platform.

The goal is to combine football data, predictions and AI insights to generate scalable football content.

---

# Content Philosophy

Foozi content is built around three principles.

Data-driven  
Short-form insights  
Automated generation

The platform should prioritize intelligent football insights instead of long editorial articles.

---

# Core Content Types

The platform generates several types of content.

Match previews  
Match reports  
Team insights  
Player insights  
Competition summaries  

These content types are powered by the data and AI insight engines.

---

# Match Previews

Generated before a match starts.

Example

"Bayern Munich face Borussia Dortmund in a crucial Bundesliga clash.

Bayern have won 5 of their last 6 home matches while Dortmund scored in their last 7 games."

Used on

match pages  
homepage highlights

---

# Match Reports

Generated after a match finishes.

Example

"Bayern Munich defeated Borussia Dortmund 3–1 at Allianz Arena.

Harry Kane scored twice while Jamal Musiala added another goal."

Used on

match pages  
competition pages

---

# Team Insights

Insights about team performance.

Examples

"Arsenal won 6 of their last 7 matches."

"Inter has the strongest defense in Serie A."

Used on

team pages  
competition pages

---

# Player Insights

Insights about player performance.

Examples

"Haaland scored in 4 consecutive matches."

"Musiala created 12 chances in the last 5 games."

Used on

player pages  
match previews

---

# Competition Insights

Insights about league-wide statistics.

Examples

"The Bundesliga averages 3.1 goals per match."

"Home teams win 54% of matches in the Premier League."

Used on

competition pages  
homepage

---

# AI Content Generation

The AI Insight Engine transforms statistical signals into natural language statements.

Signals include

goal streaks  
winning streaks  
defensive records  
xG trends  

These signals are converted into short football insights.

---

# Content Placement

Generated content appears across the platform.

Homepage  
Competition pages  
Match pages  
Team pages  
Player pages  

This ensures that every page contains contextual insights.

---

# Long-Term Expansion

Future content modules may include

match momentum analysis  
betting value picks  
player comparisons  
tactical insights  

These modules can extend the Foozi intelligence layer.

---

# Platform Goal

The Foozi content engine should provide continuous football insights powered by data and AI.
