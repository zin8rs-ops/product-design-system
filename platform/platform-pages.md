# Platform Page Structure

This document defines the page structure of the football data platform.

---

# Core Pages

## Home

Overview of major competitions and highlights.

Sections

- top competitions
- featured matches
- player rankings
- highlights
- latest news

---

# Competition Pages

Each competition has a dedicated page.

Example

/bundesliga  
/premier-league  
/champions-league  
/world-cup  

Sections

- competition hero
- match list
- standings
- player rankings
- statistics
- highlights
- news

---

# Match Pages

Individual match pages.

Example

/match/bayern-vs-dortmund

Sections

- match header
- score
- lineups
- statistics
- timeline
- highlights

---

# Team Pages

Team overview pages.

Example

/team/bayern-munich

Sections

- team overview
- squad
- recent matches
- standings
- statistics

---

# Player Pages

Individual player profiles.

Example

/player/jamal-musiala

Sections

- player overview
- statistics
- match history
- market value
- career

---

# Ranking Pages

Player rankings and statistics.

Examples

/top-scorers  
/most-assists  
/market-values  

Sections

- leaderboard
- player rows
- statistics

---

# Media Pages

Content pages for media.

Examples

/highlights  
/news  

Sections

- video carousel
- news cards
