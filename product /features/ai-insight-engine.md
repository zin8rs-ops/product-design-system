# AI Insight Engine

The AI Insight Engine generates short, data-driven insights from football statistics.

The goal is to translate complex data into understandable statements for users.

---

# Insight Types

Insights are generated from different data categories.

## Match Insights

Insights about upcoming matches.

Examples

"Bayern scored in 9 consecutive home matches."

"Dortmund conceded in 7 of the last 8 away matches."

Used on

- homepage
- match pages

---

## Team Insights

Insights about team performance.

Examples

"Arsenal won 6 of the last 7 league matches."

"Inter has the strongest defense in the league."

Used on

- team pages
- competition pages

---

## Player Insights

Insights about individual players.

Examples

"Haaland scored in 4 consecutive matches."

"Musiala created 12 chances in the last 5 games."

Used on

- player pages
- match previews

---

## Competition Insights

Insights about league-wide trends.

Examples

"The Bundesliga averages 3.1 goals per match."

"Home teams win 54% of matches in the Premier League."

Used on

- competition pages
- homepage

---

# Insight Generation

Insights are generated using statistical triggers.

Examples

Winning streak  
Goal streak  
Defensive records  
Expected goals trends  

The engine converts statistical signals into natural language insights.

---

# UX Guidelines

Insights must be:

Short  
Clear  
Data-based  

Maximum

3–5 insights per section.
