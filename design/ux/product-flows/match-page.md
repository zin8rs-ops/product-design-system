# Foozi – Match Page UX Specification

Version: 1.0  
Status: Draft  
Owner: Product / UX  

---

# Objective

The match page is the **central information hub for a specific football match**.

Users visit match pages to:

1. Check match details
2. Understand predictions and probabilities
3. Explore statistics and insights
4. View lineups and match events

Match pages should feel like an **intelligence dashboard for a single match**.

---

# Page Structure

Order of sections:

1. Match Hero
2. Tactra Prediction
3. Key Match Stats
4. Lineups
5. AI Match Insights
6. Match Timeline
7. Head-to-Head
8. Related Matches
9. Community Predictions

---

# 1 Match Hero

Purpose:

Immediately communicate the match context.

Content:

- home team
- away team
- team logos
- match date
- kickoff time
- stadium
- competition

Example:

Bayern Munich vs Borussia Dortmund  
Bundesliga – Matchday 21  
Saturday 18:30

---

# 2 Tactra Prediction

Purpose:

Show Foozi's core prediction engine.

Content:

Prediction probabilities:

Home Win  
Draw  
Away Win  

Example:

Home Win — 64%  
Draw — 21%  
Away Win — 15%

Visualization:

Prediction bar or probability chart.

---

# 3 Key Match Stats

Purpose:

Provide a quick statistical comparison.

Examples:

xG Trend  
Form Score  
Average Goals  
Defensive Rating  

Display:

2-column comparison.

Example:

Bayern vs Dortmund

xG: 2.3 — 1.8  
Form: W-W-W-D-W — W-D-W-L-W  

---

# 4 Lineups

Purpose:

Show expected or confirmed starting lineups.

Content:

- starting XI
- formations
- key players

Example:

4-2-3-1 vs 4-3-3

---

# 5 AI Match Insights

Purpose:

Explain patterns and trends in the data.

Example insights:

Bayern scored in 12 consecutive home matches.

Dortmund conceded in 7 of the last 8 away matches.

Maximum **3–4 insights**.

---

# 6 Match Timeline

Purpose:

Display key match events.

Events include:

- goals
- assists
- cards
- substitutions

Example:

12' Kane Goal  
37' Yellow Card  
65' Substitution  

---

# 7 Head-to-Head

Purpose:

Show historical context between the two teams.

Content:

- last 5 matches
- wins
- draws
- goals scored

Example:

Last 5 matches:

Bayern Wins: 3  
Draws: 1  
Dortmund Wins: 1  

---

# 8 Related Matches

Purpose:

Encourage further exploration.

Examples:

Other matches from the same matchday.

Example:

Leverkusen vs Stuttgart  
Union Berlin vs Frankfurt

---

# 9 Community Predictions

Purpose:

Engage users with predictions.

Example:

78% predict Bayern win.

CTA:

Make your prediction

---

# UX Design Principles

Hierarchy

Prediction and match context should appear above the fold.

Scanability

Users should quickly understand the match outlook.

Insight-first

Data interpretation should accompany raw statistics.

---

# Anti-Patterns

Avoid:

- long statistical tables
- overly complex visualizations
- hiding predictions below the fold

---

# Success Metrics

Match page success will be measured via:

- prediction participation rate
- time on page
- scroll depth
- match insights CTR
