# Foozi – Player Page UX Specification

Version: 1.0  
Status: Draft  
Owner: Product / UX  

---

# Objective

The player page provides a detailed overview of a football player.

Users visit player pages to:

1. Check statistics
2. Explore performance trends
3. View match history
4. Understand player impact

Player pages should function as **player intelligence dashboards**.

---

# Page Structure

Order of sections:

1. Player Hero
2. Season Statistics
3. Recent Matches
4. Performance Trends
5. AI Player Insights
6. Career Overview
7. Related Players

---

# 1 Player Hero

Purpose:

Introduce the player.

Content:

- player photo
- name
- position
- team
- nationality
- age

Example:

Jamal Musiala  
Midfielder – Bayern Munich

---

# 2 Season Statistics

Purpose:

Show key performance metrics.

Examples:

Goals  
Assists  
Matches played  
Expected goals (xG)

Display:

Stat cards.

---

# 3 Recent Matches

Purpose:

Highlight player contributions.

Example:

Last 5 matches:

Goal vs Stuttgart  
Assist vs Leipzig

---

# 4 Performance Trends

Purpose:

Show performance development.

Examples:

Goal frequency  
Rating trend  
Minutes played

Display:

Small charts.

---

# 5 AI Player Insights

Purpose:

Explain player impact.

Example insights:

Musiala created 12 chances in the last 5 matches.

Haaland scored in 4 consecutive matches.

---

# 6 Career Overview

Purpose:

Show career progression.

Content:

Previous clubs  
Appearances  
Goals

---

# 7 Related Players

Purpose:

Encourage exploration.

Examples:

Similar players  
Teammates  
League rivals
