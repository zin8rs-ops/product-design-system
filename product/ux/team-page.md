# Foozi – Team Page UX Specification

Version: 1.0  
Status: Draft  
Owner: Product / UX  

---

# Objective

The team page provides a comprehensive overview of a football club.

Users visit team pages to:

1. Explore the squad
2. Check recent performance
3. View team statistics
4. Discover upcoming matches

Team pages should feel like a **team intelligence dashboard**.

---

# Page Structure

Order of sections:

1. Team Hero
2. Next Match
3. Recent Matches
4. Squad
5. Team Statistics
6. AI Team Insights
7. Competition Position
8. Related Players

---

# 1 Team Hero

Purpose:

Provide immediate team context.

Content:

- team logo
- team name
- stadium
- coach
- league

Example:

Bayern Munich  
Allianz Arena  
Bundesliga

---

# 2 Next Match

Purpose:

Highlight the upcoming game.

Content:

Match card:

- opponent
- date
- competition
- tactra prediction

Example:

Bayern vs Dortmund  
Saturday 18:30

---

# 3 Recent Matches

Purpose:

Show recent team performance.

Display:

Last 5 matches.

Example:

W – W – D – W – L

Each match card links to match page.

---

# 4 Squad

Purpose:

Show all players.

Grouped by:

Goalkeepers  
Defenders  
Midfielders  
Forwards

Each player row includes:

- player photo
- name
- position
- number

Each player links to player page.

---

# 5 Team Statistics

Purpose:

Provide key performance metrics.

Examples:

Average goals per match  
Clean sheets  
Possession rate  
Shots per match

Display:

Stat cards.

---

# 6 AI Team Insights

Purpose:

Highlight interesting team trends.

Example insights:

Bayern scored in the last 9 home matches.

Dortmund conceded only 3 goals in the last 6 games.

Limit insights to **3–4 cards**.

---

# 7 Competition Position

Purpose:

Provide league context.

Content:

Mini league table.

Highlight current team position.

Example:

Position: 2nd  
Points: 51

---

# 8 Related Players

Purpose:

Highlight key players.

Examples:

Top scorer  
Most assists  
Highest rated player

Each card links to player page.

---

# UX Design Principles

Exploration

Team pages should guide users toward players and matches.

Clarity

Team performance must be understandable at a glance.

Hierarchy

Upcoming matches should appear before squad details.

---

# Success Metrics

Team page performance will be measured via:

- player page CTR
- match page navigation
- session depth
