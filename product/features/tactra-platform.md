# Tactra Platform

Tactra is the prediction and intelligence system of the Foozi platform.

The goal of Tactra is to provide interpretable match predictions powered by football data and AI insights.

---

# Core Tactra Modules

Tactra Prediction  
Tactra Confidence  
Tactra Signals  
Tactra Insights  
Tactra Picks  

These modules work together to provide match intelligence.

---

# 1 Tactra Prediction

Predicts the outcome probability of a match.

Output

Home Win  
Draw  
Away Win  

Example

Bayern vs Dortmund

Home Win — 64%  
Draw — 21%  
Away Win — 15%

---

# 2 Tactra Confidence

Indicates how reliable the prediction is.

Example

Confidence Score

82%

Signals

team stability  
data reliability  
injury uncertainty  

---

# 3 Tactra Signals

Signals explain the key statistical factors behind the prediction.

Example

Strong home form  
Higher xG trend  
Opponent defensive injuries  

Signals help users understand the prediction.

---

# 4 Tactra Insights

AI-generated explanations based on statistical patterns.

Example

"Bayern scored in 9 consecutive home matches."

Insights translate raw data into human-readable analysis.

---

# 5 Tactra Picks

Tactra highlights matches with strong prediction signals.

Example

Tactra Pick

Bayern vs Dortmund  
Prediction: Home Win  
Confidence: 84%

These picks appear on the homepage and match pages.
